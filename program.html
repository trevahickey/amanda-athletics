Provided Programs

Running

In this program, members will have the opportunity to go on runs of varying distances with Coach A. There will be distances of 5K and up as well as timed runs such as a quick 20 minutes or longer hour. Additionally, Amanda will help coach through different terrains as well as different interval training.

Zumba

In this upbeat interval workout, utilizing Latin-inspird dance moves and fun music, you'll be sure to have a blast while working out! We have always said that fitness should be a fun and enjoyable experience. With this workout, we can nearly guarantee you'll leave with a smile on your face.

Weight Training

In this program, we have levels from beginner to advanced and everywhere inbetween. This integrates the teachings of how to appropriately lift weights and use resistance training to get the best workout possible, and a generalized workout that can be personalized to best suit the individual.

Yoga

In our yoga program, we want to emphasize the mind, body, breath connection. Not only is this an excellent workout, but it is also a chance to reset and reframe. We aim to create a positive, safe space where individuals can get whatever it is they need out of the spiritual practice.
